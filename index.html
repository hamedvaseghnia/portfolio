Welcome! I am Hamed Vaseghnia, Ph.D. candidate in Computational Engineering at the University of Stavanger.

Mechanical Engineer and
Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) researcher.

I am passionate about the fascinating world of fluid mechanics, with a keen interest in the modeling and simulation of complex fluid dynamics systems.

My expertise primarily lies in multiphase flow, heat transfer, and non-Newtonian fluid flows, areas that present unique challenges and opportunities for discovery. My research focuses on utilizing advanced numerical and mathematical techniques to explore the intricate dynamics of physcial phenomena in fluid flows.

I aim to apply my knowledge in computational fluid mechanics to benefit various industries. Whether it's improving industrial processes, improving health conditions, or advancing technological innovations.
